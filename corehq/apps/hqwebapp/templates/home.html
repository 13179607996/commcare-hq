Supporting the health of communities around the world

75,000 patient visits - 14 projects worldwide - 500 registered CHWs

What is CommCare?

  • An open-source software application
  • A job aid for community health workers (CHWs)
  • A tool for supervision and evidence-based change
  • A way to capture data in an electronic repository that otherwise sits in thousands of paper notebooks

Why CommCare?

  • CHWs have demonstrated a potential to substantially improve health outcomes in areas of extreme poverty
  • CHWs tend to have little training and operate independently
  • CommCare provides phone-based tools that help support and manage large numbers of CHWs

Highlights

  • Runs on mid-range cell phones
  • Supports images and audio
  • Handles multiple languages
    the pregnancy module is currently in English, Spanish, and Swahili
